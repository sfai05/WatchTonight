Overview

WatchTonight ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website watchtonight.app. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you visit the site, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

WatchTonight is a read-only content discovery tool. We do not offer user accounts, user-generated content, or social features. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Information We Collect

1. Information you provide

We do not ask you to create an account or submit personal information. No forms, logins, or sign-ups are offered on this site.

2. Automatically collected information

When you visit WatchTonight, standard web server and analytics infrastructure may automatically collect:

  • Your browser type and version
  • Operating system and device type
  • Pages visited and time spent on the site
  • Referring URL (the page you came from)
  • IP address (anonymised where possible)

This information is used solely to understand how visitors use the site and to improve performance and content relevance.

3. Cookies and local storage

We use small cookies and browser storage to remember your preferences across visits, such as your preferred colour theme and whether you have dismissed the hero banner. These are first-party, functional cookies — they are not used for advertising or tracking.

You can clear these preferences at any time by clearing your browser cookies and site data for watchtonight.app.

How We Use Information

We use collected information only to:

  • Operate and improve the WatchTonight website
  • Understand aggregate usage patterns (e.g. which content is popular)
  • Remember your display preferences between visits
  • Investigate and fix technical issues

We do not use your information to build advertising profiles, sell to data brokers, or target you with marketing.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services. We encourage you to review their respective privacy policies.

Server access logs, if retained, are kept for no longer than 90 days and are then automatically purged. Preference cookies stored in your browser persist until you clear them or they expire (up to 1 year).

WatchTonight is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted personal information to us, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.
